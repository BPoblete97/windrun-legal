Privacy Policy – WindRun

Last updated: May 6, 2026

WindRun ("we", "our", or "us"), developed by Emmary Studio, operates the WindRun mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your name and email address via Firebase Authentication.
  • Location data (GPS): We collect GPS location during active running sessions to record distance and route. Location data is only collected while a run is in progress.
  • Usage data: We collect anonymous analytics data (e.g. sessions completed, features used) via Firebase Analytics to improve the App.
  • Run history: Session data (distance, time, XP earned, route) is stored in your account on Firebase Firestore to power the leaderboard and personal progress features.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's core features (GPS tracking, XP progression, leaderboard).
  • To sync your data across devices via your Google account.
  • To send local notifications (daily reminders) if you enable them. We do not send marketing emails.
  • To improve the App through anonymous usage analytics.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your data is shared only with:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, database, analytics) — governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Maps — used to render your run route on a map.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

WindRun is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

We use Firebase's industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: windrun.app@gmail.com
